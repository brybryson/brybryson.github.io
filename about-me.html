About Me
Bryant’s Portfolio

Hello, I'm Bryant Melliza, currently serving as the Chief Creative Officer at the Google Developer Students Club - National University Fairview. My passion lies in transforming intricate challenges into elegant and user-friendly designs. I believe in the power of simplicity and beauty to enhance user experiences, and I strive to create intuitive designs that make complex problems more accessible.

I am currently pursuing my education at National University Fairview, where I have consistently earned the Dean's Lister recognition throughout my first year, maintaining my commitment to academic excellence. My academic journey traces back to Caloocan National Science and Technology High School and Union Village Christian Academy, where I laid the foundations for my future endeavors.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have achieved notable distinctions, such as being a consistent Dona Miguela M. Jhocson Scholar and earning high honors with a Silver Seal of Excellence in my Grade 12 year. Beyond academics, I've showcased my creativity by securing awards in competitions like DSPC 2019, where I earned 2nd Place for Best Page Photo.

Outside the academic realm, I am an avid animal lover who enjoys the simple pleasures of life, including savoring delicious meals, indulging in restful sleep, and immersing myself in the world of games. Despite life's challenges, I maintain a resilient spirit and live by the philosophy that tasting defeat serves as a valuable lesson, propelling us to become better individuals and ultimately achieving triumph in our pursuits. As I navigate through my journey, I am determined to succeed and make a positive impact in the ever-evolving field of UI/UX design.
